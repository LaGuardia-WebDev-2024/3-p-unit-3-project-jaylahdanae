Ingredients Quantity
Oxtails Handfull
rice 2 cups
seasoning (jerk,obay,oxtail & etc Until you feel the need to stop
butter 1 stick

Step 1: Wash your hands.

Step 2: wash the oxtails.

Step 3: season and oil the oxtails.

Step 4: Brown the oxtails and set the oil on heat medium.

Step 5: Set the oxtails aside, and began on the oxtail broth. Add broth, water, vegtables, and spices.

Step 6: Add Oxtails to the flavorful pot of seasonings, butter and spices and let it boil for about 2 and 1/2 hours.

Step 7:Began to add vetables like, onions , potatoes, and carrots to the pot with the oxtails.

Step 8:Boil rice adding sugar, and butter Gently stir oxtails evry 5 minutes.

Step 9: Serve the oxtails on the rice with broths on the rice and vegtables on the side.
